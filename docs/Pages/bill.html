21st Century Regulation of Information Technology Act

Cyber laws pertain to any law that deals with the internet's relationship to electronic elements, including computers, software, hardware and information systems (IS).
A technology company (or tech company) is an electronics-based technology company, including, for example, business relating to digital electronics, software, and internet-related services, such as e-commerce services.

Columbia High School, Participation in Government Assembly

East Greenbush, New York

May 25, 2021

Mr. Christian Cirincione, Mr. Jacob Martin, and Mr. Ryan Johnson introduce the following bill:


Create a government agency that solely regulates and enforces cyber laws at a national level


21st Century Regulation of Information Technology Act


Section 1:

   Create a government agency that solely regulates and enforces cyber laws at a national level


Section 2:

   For the purposes of this bill the following definitions should apply:

   (a)Cyber laws Refers to the rules you have to follow on online platforms and services.

   elements, including computers, software, hardware and information systems (IS).

   (b)Tech Companies are electronics-based technology company, including, for example, business

   relating to digital electronics, software, and internet-related services, such as e-commerce services.

   (c)Federal Department of Internet Regulation (FDIR)


Section 3:

   This government agency will be established as the Federal Department of Internet Regulation, as

   an executive department of the United States. The primary mission of the FDIR will be to create, and

   enforce cyber laws as well as protect the general public's data from tech companies.


Section: 4

   This bill is going to create a government agency that solely regulates and enforces cyber laws

   at a national level. The primary goal of this new government agency is to regulate and enforce

   stricter laws on Tech Companies . In order to reduce Tech Companies's influence on the general public and

   create a safer environment for people using the internet, this bill is necessary. In addition to creating a safer

   environment this bill will grant the FDIR access to review and edit Tech Companies TOS

   in the effort to remove censorship put in place by these companies in the hopes to create an internet environment that represents

   everyone equally.


Section: 5

   This bill will be funded by the revenue acquired from increasing taxes on Tech Companiesh. With the intent on using the

   money to build and enforce the FDIR government agency regulating the control of Tech Companies over

   information dealt out and held from the general public.


Section: 6

   Failure to follow these regulations set in place by the FDIR will result in heavy

   fines of up to 50% of the companies annual income, and/or jail time should the people held

   responsible continue to disregard the regulations.


Section: 7

   This bill shall go into effect one year upon approval (or on January,1 2022).
